# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Clap to Open to be a friendly, harassment-free space for everyone,
regardless of experience level or background.

## Be excellent to each other

- Be respectful, patient, and welcoming — especially to first-time contributors.
- Assume good faith; critique ideas, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Scope & enforcement

This applies in all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions). Report
unacceptable behavior by opening a confidential
[security advisory](https://github.com/Forrest404/clap-to-open/security/advisories/new)
or contacting the maintainer. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, or
contributors that violate this code.

This is a condensed adaptation of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), v2.1.
